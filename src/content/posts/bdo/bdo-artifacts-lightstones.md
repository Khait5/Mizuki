# BDO 2025 Artifacts and Lightstones Guide: Maximize Your Stats

Artifacts and Lightstones offer powerful passive stat boosts and set effects, acting as a final layer of optimization for your character.

You can equip two Artifacts. They provide a base stat and two Lightstone slots each.

* **Artifacts of the Boss Slayer:** Generally best for PvE, giving Monster Damage Reduction or Monster AP.
* **Artifact of the Hunter:** Good for PvP, giving high damage reduction or PvP damage.
* **Artifacts of the Florin:** For Life Skills, providing buffs to all life skill mastery.
