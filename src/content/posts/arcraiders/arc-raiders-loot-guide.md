# Arc Raiders: Important Loot vs. Recycling Trash

Loot management is crucial in an extraction shooter. Knowing what to keep and what to recycle will save you time and maximize your profits.

## ‚≠ê Keepers (Important Loot)

* **Weapon Mods (Legendary/Epic):** These are game-changers. Look for mods that add extra elemental damage or increase critical hit chance.
* **Blueprints:** Permanent crafting recipes that allow you to bring in better gear on future drops.
* **High-Tier Raw Materials (T3 and up):** Needed for crafting your best base gear. Always prioritize **Iridium** and **Synth-Fiber**.
* **Data Drives:** Used for research and unlocking new Raider abilities.

* **Common (White) Armor Components:** Once you have a strong inventory, these are low-value and should be recycled for raw materials.
* **Basic Medical Supplies:** Keep a stack of 3-5, but recycle any excess.
