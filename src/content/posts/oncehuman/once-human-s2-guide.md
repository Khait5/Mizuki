# Once Human: Season 2 'The Deep Cold' Survival Guide

Season 2 introduces the winter biome and a new challenge: extreme temperature survival and ice-based Deviants.

## ❄️ New Mechanic: Hypothermia

You now need specialized survival gear and food to maintain core temperature. Crafting the **Thermal Insulation Suit** is the highest priority.

## ⚔️ Seasonal Boss: The Frostbeast

* **Location:** The Frozen Lake (High Threat Zone).
* **Abilities:** Summons ice pillars and inflicts guaranteed **Slow** debuff.
* **Loot:** The **Frost Shard**, used to craft the powerful **Tidal Rifle**.
